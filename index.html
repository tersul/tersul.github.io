Introducing StaffID™

StaffID™ is a secure workforce management and collaboration solution designed for frontline and shift-based workers. It combines secure sign-in, push notifications, shift scheduling, clock-in and clock-out, task management, timesheets and more, without needing an in-house IT team.

Secure Authentication

Simple, memorable sign-in for workers with low IT literacy who are accessing workplace apps on any device, backed by strong identity protection.

BYOD Ready

Protection policies keep work data secure inside the apps that staff use on their own devices.

Time & Attendance

Built-in clock-in and clock-out, shift scheduling and automated timesheet generation for accurate, efficient payroll.

Task Management

Assign one-off and recurring tasks to individuals or teams, with clear status tracking and completion notifications.

Team Communication

Real-time messaging, team channels and instant push notifications to workers' personal mobile devices.

Rapid Deployment

Get your frontline workers connected and productive quickly with a streamlined onboarding process that keeps IT overhead low.
